Our policies

Prevention policies against child labor

AGUALIMA S.A.C. is against child labor, does not allow the hiring of minors and does everything possible to eradicate it; It is considered a serious fault so we consider immediate and exemplary corrective measures to who contravenes this policy.

The remediation procedure in case minors are detected working at the company includes removing them from the workplace and reporting to the child protection entity (DEMUNA).

Subsequently, the company is committed to follow up to promote the welfare of the minor.

Policies against political discrimination of free work and without abuse

AGUALIMA S.A.C. is committed to providing a workplace free of discrimination and harassment. Whoever commits any of these acts will be sanctioned according to the provisions contained in the Internal Work Regulations.

The company guarantees that the people who work in AGUALIMA S.A.C. be treated with kindness and respect without judging or discriminating on the basis of race, color, religion, opinion, economic status, sex, nationality, age, disability or any other nature.

Similarly, AGUALIMA S.A.C. is committed to offering its workers a work environment free of violence, threats, intimidation or any form of abuse. The company does not participate in or tolerate corporal punishment, mental or physical coercion, verbal abuse or other forms of abusive disciplinary measures.

The company establishes as a policy that any worker can file a claim following the labor complaint procedure and report any fact that constitutes some form of abuse; committing ourselves to eradicate behaviors that contravene this policy and without retaliation.

Likewise, the company guarantees free work. Any form of forced labor is prohibited. Any worker has the freedom to resign from his job if he so decides. In addition, AGUALIMA S.A.C., ensures that workers enjoy freedom of movement at all times, without imposing disciplinary sanctions.

Our anti-bribery and anti-corruption policy

AGUALIMA S.A.C is committed to working with integrity, transparency, guaranteeing efficiency; under an ethical and transparent organizational culture, of respect for Anti- bribery laws and regulations, whose compliance is promoted through each worker, as well as through its administrative bodies, undertaking to denounce any act of corruption without fear of reprisals and to continuously improve its anti-bribery management system.

The company AGUALIMA SAC is not allowed to pay, offer, accept or receive a bribe in any way, or encourage and / or participate directly or indirectly in acts of corruption.
